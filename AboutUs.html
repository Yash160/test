About Us

NeuroFit: Your Path to Mental Wellness

NeuroFit is a mental health platform designed to empower users to prioritize their well-being. Created by college students, it offers a variety of resources including self-assessments, a therapist directory, and a relaxation blog. Whether you're a student, working professional, or anyone seeking to improve your mental health, NeuroFit provides a one-stop shop to support your journey.

Join the NeuroFit community to connect with others and share your experiences in a supportive environment.
